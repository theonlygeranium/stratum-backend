# About EdStratum Labs

EdStratum Labs is a boutique, founder-led AI strategy and implementation consultancy based in San Francisco, California, serving organizations worldwide. The company delivers verifiable AI implementation for EdTech platforms and growth-stage organizations. EdStratum builds on evidence, not hype, and ships production-grade systems that teams can maintain after launch.

## What EdStratum Does

EdStratum helps organizations implement AI in education technology contexts. The core service areas are Canvas LMS integration, AI implementation strategy, production AI and RAG engineering, learning analytics, AI workflow automation, and fractional AI leadership. The work spans from early-stage strategy and roadmaps through to production deployment and operational handoff.

EdStratum is not a generic chatbot vendor, an ad agency, or a broad automation marketplace. The focus is specifically on EdTech, Canvas, RAG, learning data, and AI systems that need to be grounded in real institutional data.

## Who EdStratum Serves

EdStratum works with EdTech platforms, higher education institutions, K-12 organizations, and growth-stage companies that need AI implementation expertise. Clients typically have a concrete workflow problem, some form of data or platform infrastructure, and a need for production-grade AI systems rather than prototypes or slide decks.

## Founder-Led Model

EdStratum is founder-led. Jeffrey Geronimo, the founder, works directly with clients. There are no account managers, no automated sales sequences, and no handoffs to junior staff. The first conversation is a focused discovery call with Jeffrey, not a qualification screening.

## Engagement Model

The first conversation is a focused discovery call. The goal is to define the problem accurately before writing a proposal. EdStratum emphasizes direct, substantive responses and practical implementation over abstract strategy. The default recommendation is a focused discovery audit before any build commitment.

The engagement model is designed to avoid expensive experimentation without evidence. EdStratum resists full-stack retainers when a targeted fix is sufficient. A useful AI initiative should have a concrete workflow, stable data access, an integration path, and a measurable outcome.

## Professional Services

Yes, EdStratum offers professional services. The services include AI implementation strategy consulting, Canvas LMS integration development, production RAG pipeline engineering, learning analytics implementation, AI workflow automation, and fractional AI leadership. Engagements can range from a short discovery audit to a full production build and deployment.

## Evidence-Driven Philosophy

EdStratum's operating philosophy is layered architecture: strategy, implementation, and evidence each need to support the next layer. AI initiatives fail when foundational layers are weak, so engagements are scoped to the layer that needs work. Strategy and implementation are treated as separate but connected layers: strategy defines the evidence threshold, implementation ships the smallest maintainable system that can prove or disprove the plan.

## Core Strengths

The public positioning describes core strengths in Instructure Canvas, enterprise AI, LLM integration, custom plugin development, conversational AI and voice agent design, customer education systems, RAG architecture, and AI implementation for EdTech.
