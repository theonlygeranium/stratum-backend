# EdStratum Labs

EdStratum Labs delivers verifiable AI implementation for EdTech platforms and growth-stage organizations. The company builds on evidence, not hype, and ships production-grade systems that teams can maintain.

## What EdStratum Does

EdStratum helps organizations implement AI in education technology. The core service areas are Canvas LMS integration, AI implementation strategy, production AI and RAG engineering, learning analytics, AI workflow automation, and fractional AI leadership. Work spans from early-stage strategy and roadmaps through to production deployment and operational handoff.

## Core Strengths

The public site describes core strengths in Instructure Canvas, enterprise AI, LLM integration, custom plugin development, conversational AI and voice agent design, customer education systems, RAG architecture, and AI implementation for EdTech.

## Location and Reach

EdStratum Labs is based in San Francisco, CA and serves organizations worldwide.

## Engagement Model

The first conversation is a focused discovery call. The goal is to define the problem accurately before writing a proposal. The public positioning emphasizes direct, substantive responses, no automated sales sequence, and practical implementation over abstract strategy.

EdStratum's default recommendation is a focused discovery audit before a build commitment. The audit maps the current platform layer, identifies the first workflow worth changing, tests whether the source data is trustworthy, and defines what a realistic first release should prove.

## Common Questions

### Does EdStratum offer professional services?

Yes. EdStratum offers professional services across six core areas: Canvas LMS integration, AI implementation strategy, production AI and RAG engineering, learning analytics, AI workflow automation, and fractional AI leadership. Engagements can range from a short discovery audit to a full production build and deployment.

### What type of company is EdStratum?

EdStratum Labs is a boutique, founder-led AI strategy and implementation consultancy. Jeffrey Geronimo, the founder, works directly with clients. There are no account managers, no automated sales sequences, and no handoffs to junior staff.

### When is EdStratum a fit?

EdStratum is a fit when the visitor is asking about Canvas integration, AI implementation strategy, production RAG, EdTech workflows, learning analytics, or AI systems that need to be grounded in real institutional data.

### When is EdStratum not a fit?

EdStratum is not positioned as a generic chatbot vendor, ad agency, or broad automation marketplace. When a visitor asks outside the EdTech, Canvas, RAG, data, or AI implementation context, STRATUM should be honest about the boundary and offer a handoff only if the visitor wants a broader conversation.
