# EdStratum Service Areas

## Canvas LMS Integration

EdStratum builds purpose-built plugins, custom API workflows, and LTI integrations that extend Instructure Canvas beyond out-of-the-box limits. Relevant work includes custom LTI 1.3 tool development, Canvas Data 2 pipeline architecture, gradebook automation and reporting, single sign-on, and roster sync.

Good fits include teams that need a custom Canvas workflow, a safer LTI launch path, grade passback, roster-aware user experiences, reporting pipelines, or a bridge between Canvas and another product system.

## AI Implementation Strategy

EdStratum creates grounded, evidence-driven roadmaps that identify where AI creates measurable ROI and where it does not. Relevant work includes LLM selection, evaluation frameworks, build-versus-buy analysis, total cost of ownership models, responsible AI policy scaffolding, risk review, and compliance audit trails.

Good fits include executives or product leaders who need a practical AI roadmap, vendor review, pilot design, data readiness assessment, governance baseline, or a clear stop/go decision before investing in production engineering.

## Production AI Engineering

EdStratum develops AI-powered features from prompt architecture and RAG pipelines to production deployment and observability. Relevant work includes vector database setup, retrieval evaluation, API integration, cost optimization, logging, model monitoring, and maintainable release patterns.

Good fits include teams that already know the use case and need a production-grade RAG pipeline, tool-calling workflow, evaluation harness, prompt system, structured output integration, or operational monitoring.

## Learning Analytics and Insights

EdStratum transforms LMS event data into intelligence that surfaces intervention signals early and measures whether instruction changes outcomes. Relevant work includes xAPI or Caliper learner event pipelines, predictive engagement scoring, custom dashboards, and content testing frameworks.

## AI Workflow Automation

EdStratum identifies high-friction administrative workflows and replaces them with reliable, auditable AI pipelines. Example workflows include syllabus parsing, curriculum mapping, rubric generation, approval routing, notification operations, document intelligence, and extraction.

## Fractional AI Leadership

EdStratum provides senior AI expertise on demand for organizations that need architectural review, vendor evaluation, RFP support, team skill development, board briefings, and executive AI guidance without a full-time AI executive.

This service is most useful when a team has internal delivery capacity but needs senior judgment, a second opinion on architecture, or a disciplined path from idea to production decision.
